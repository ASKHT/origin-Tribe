WHY US

At Origins Tribe, we believe that an individual’s switch to a more sustainable lifestyle is a precursor to achieving societal sustainability—and the products he or she uses should reflect this. We hope that by selling a variety of products that are environmentally friendly and beautiful, we can convince people to make the switch without sacrificing what they love.

WHO ARE WE

Origin tribe is a community of people who share sustainable values. We are also a marketplace offering 100% sustainable products for gifting, events and everyday living.
